Rental Agreement

This Deed of Agreement is made at Faisalabad on this [Insert Date] day of March, 2026. BETWEEN [Landlord Name], s/o [Father's Name], Resident of [Address], holding CNIC No: [CNIC], hereinafter referred to as the LANDLORD (First Party). AND [Tenant Name], s/o [Father's Name], Resident of [Permanent Address], holding CNIC No: [CNIC], hereinafter referred to as the TENANT (Second Party). TERMS AND CONDITIONS 1. Minimum Residency & Security Forfeiture: The Tenant is bound by a minimum stay of one (1) year. If the Tenant vacates the property for any reason before completing the full 12-month term—regardless of whether a notice period is provided or not—the total Security Deposit shall be forfeited in favor of the Landlord and will not be refunded. 2. Annual Rent Increase: The monthly rent shall automatically increase by 10% at the completion of every 12-month cycle from the date of signing. 3. Monthly Rent & Late Penalties: Rent is payable in advance by the 10th of every month. If payment is delayed beyond the 15th, a penalty of 7% of the total monthly rent will be charged. If the Tenant fails to pay, the amount will be deducted from the security deposit. If the security is exhausted, the Tenant must vacate immediately. 4. Security Deposit Structure: The total Security Deposit is a fixed amount equal to two (2) months' rent. The payment schedule for this deposit is as follows: • First Half (Equal to 1 month's rent): Must be paid within the first 30 days of tenancy. • Second Half (Equal to 1 month's rent): Must be paid in full by the end of the third (3rd) month of tenancy. • Strict Penalty: If the Tenant fails to complete the second half of the security payment by the end of the third month, the first half already paid will be automatically voided (forfeited), and the Tenant must vacate the property immediately. 5. Utility Bill Verification: The Tenant is responsible for all utility charges (Electricity, Gas, Water, Trash). The Tenant must provide proof of paid bills to the Landlord by the 10th of every month. Failure to show paid receipts is a breach of contract. 6. Bi-Annual Safety & Compliance Inspection: The Landlord reserves the right to enter and inspect the property every six (6) months to ensure safety, structural integrity, and compliance. The Tenant is obligated to facilitate this inspection. 7. Inspection & Video Evidence: The Tenant confirms the property is in pristine condition. A complete video recording has been established as evidence. No future claims regarding pre-existing damages will be entertained. 8. Maintenance & On-Going Repairs: All damages occurring during the tenancy (plumbing, electrical, woodwork, paint, etc.) must be repaired by the Tenant at their own expense immediately. The Landlord is not responsible for any repair or maintenance costs during the stay. 9. Final Settlement: The Security Deposit will be refunded two (2) months after the property is vacated (only after the 1-year minimum is met). The Landlord will deduct final unpaid bills and costs for any damages found during the final exit inspection. 10. Notice Period: A two-month (2) written notice is mandatory before vacating the property (applicable only after the first year has been completed). 11. Legal Compliance: No Afghan nationals are allowed. The Tenant must be a Pakistani citizen with a valid CNIC and must complete Police Khidmat Markaz verification within 3 days. SIGNATURES
